2161 Post-Cascade Era

The Cascade Event

In 2161, the world changed forever. The IEZ Cascade created a 200-kilometer radius interdiction zone—a quarantined city-zone now known as Bloom. What was once civilization became a contested wasteland, reshaped by alien technology and the remnants of the mysterious Harvesters.

The Harvesters, absent alien extractors, left behind wreckage that defies understanding: Skimmer-class survey arrays, weaponized Harrower-class systems, and reality-bending Monolith-class field manipulators. These artifacts don't just power technology—they reshape the very nature of reality within Bloom.

The Art of Extraction

Survival in Bloom

In the post-Cascade world, extraction isn't just about getting out alive—it's about timing, faction politics, and understanding the alien forces that reshape reality around you.

Every operation is a calculated risk. Contested evac windows mean multiple factions may be vying for the same extraction point. Dynamic hazards shift based on Harvester artifact interference. Weather patterns, power grid stability, alarm states, and convoy schedules all factor into whether you'll make it out with your prize—or your life.

The most successful operators understand that in Bloom, reputation matters. Aid a faction and gain access to better equipment and intel. Betray them, and you'll find yourself facing coordinated opposition. Some choose arbitration, playing factions against each other. Others prefer direct confrontation—ambush tactics that leave no witnesses.
