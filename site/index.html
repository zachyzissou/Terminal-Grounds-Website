Lore Primer: What Is Bloom?

The Cascade

An event that fractured reality across a 200km Interdiction Exclusion Zone (IEZ). Physics bends. Signals ghost. Time drifts. The city was quarantined and then carved into territory.

Harvesters

Absent alien extractors whose wrecks‚ÄîSkimmer arrays, Harrower systems, and Monolith field manipulators‚Äîstill run without masters. Their artifacts change the rules of every fight.

Seven Factions

Order vs. survival vs. knowledge. From the Sky Bastion Directorate to the Iron Scavengers and Archive Keepers, alliances shift with every convoy, raid, and arbitration.

Extraction

Operations are timed, contested, and political. Hazards escalate. Reputation matters. Get in, get the artifact, get out‚Äîbefore the next bloom.

How Bloom Works

1) Plan

Pick a region (IEZ core, Machine Grave, Metro networks). Scout power states, alarms, convoy routes, and faction presence.

2) Breach

Navigate distortions, power cycles, and patrol logic. Field-class artifacts help; Splice-class can backfire; Monolith-class changes everything.

3) Extract

Evac windows are limited and contested. Aid, arbitrate, or ambush factions to control the route. Miss the window‚Äîyou stay.

4) Escalate

Bring your winnings back into the loop: tech upgrades, faction reputation, and intel unlock the next tier of risk and reward.
